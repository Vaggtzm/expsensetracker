How to use Expense Tracker

First add your current budget in the budget input, then go and add the name of the product that you spend money on and the price.

After you add all the necessary data, click the "Check Amount" button. After clicking the button, you will see a list with the name of the product, the price you previously added, and the balance of your budget or what is left from your budget after the expense.

If you want to use the Expense Tracker again, copy and paste the balance as your new budget and fill in the inputs again. Click the "Check Amount" button to see the results.
